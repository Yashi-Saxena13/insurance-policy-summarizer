SECTION 1 — COVERAGE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1.1 Hospitalization Expenses
Coverage for inpatient hospitalization expenses where the insured
is admitted for more than 24 consecutive hours.
Covered expenses include:
- Room rent up to ₹3,000 per day (single AC room)
- ICU charges up to ₹6,000 per day
- Surgeon, anaesthetist, and specialist fees
- Nursing charges
- OT charges and medicines consumed during hospitalization
- Diagnostic tests conducted during hospitalization
Maximum limit: ₹5,00,000 per policy year

1.2 Day Care Procedures
Medical procedures that require less than 24 hours of hospitalization
due to technological advancements are covered.
Covered day care procedures include:
- Cataract surgery
- Chemotherapy and radiotherapy
- Dialysis
- Lithotripsy (kidney stone removal)
- Tonsillectomy
- Angiography
Maximum limit: Up to Sum Insured (₹5,00,000)

1.3 Pre-Hospitalization Expenses
Medical expenses incurred up to 30 days before the date of
hospitalization are covered, provided they are related to the
same illness or injury for which hospitalization occurred.
Maximum limit: Up to Sum Insured

1.4 Post-Hospitalization Expenses
Medical expenses incurred up to 60 days after discharge from
hospital are covered, provided they are related to the same
illness or injury.
Maximum limit: Up to Sum Insured

1.5 Ambulance Cover
Emergency ambulance charges for transporting the insured to
the nearest hospital are covered.
Maximum limit: ₹2,000 per hospitalization

1.6 Organ Donor Expenses
Medical expenses incurred by the organ donor during harvesting
of the organ for transplant to the insured are covered.
Maximum limit: ₹50,000

1.7 AYUSH Treatment
Hospitalization expenses under Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha,
and Homeopathy (AYUSH) in a government-recognized institution.
Maximum limit: ₹25,000 per policy year

SECTION 3 — EXCLUSIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The following are NOT covered under this policy:

3.1 Pre-Existing Diseases
Any illness, condition, or disease diagnosed or treated within
48 months prior to the inception of this policy is excluded
for the first 48 months of continuous coverage.

3.2 Waiting Period Exclusions
- General waiting period: 30 days from policy start date
  (except for accidental injuries)
- Specific illness waiting period: 24 months for
  hernia, cataract, joint replacement, and varicose veins

3.3 Dental Treatment
Any dental treatment, dental surgery, or orthodontic procedures
unless necessitated by an accidental injury requiring
hospitalization.

3.4 Cosmetic and Aesthetic Procedures
- Cosmetic surgery or plastic surgery
- Weight loss treatments or bariatric surgery
  (unless BMI exceeds 40 and deemed medically necessary)
- Hair loss treatments

3.5 Maternity and Newborn
- Maternity expenses including normal and caesarean delivery
- Newborn baby expenses (unless add-on maternity cover is purchased)
- Infertility treatments and IVF

3.6 Mental Health and Substance Abuse
- Psychiatric disorders and mental illness
  (unless mandated under IRDAI guidelines)
- Alcohol or drug abuse and related complications

3.7 Adventure and Hazardous Activities
- Injuries sustained while participating in adventure sports
  such as skydiving, bungee jumping, rock climbing, or racing

3.8 War and Nuclear Risks
- Injuries or illnesses caused by war, terrorism, nuclear
  radiation, or civil unrest

3.9 Self-Inflicted Injuries
- Injuries resulting from attempted suicide or
  intentional self-harm

3.10 Non-Allopathic Treatment
- Experimental treatments or unproven medical procedures
- Treatments not recognized by the Medical Council of India

SECTION 5 — CLAIM PROCESS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

5.1 Cashless Claims (Network Hospitals)
1. Inform the insurer or TPA at least 48 hours before
   planned hospitalization (or within 24 hours for emergencies).
2. Present your health card at the hospital's insurance desk.
3. Hospital submits pre-authorization form to TPA.
4. TPA approves or rejects within 2 hours for emergencies,
   6 hours for planned hospitalization.
5. On discharge, insured pays only the non-covered amounts.

5.2 Reimbursement Claims (Non-Network Hospitals)
1. Inform the insurer within 24 hours of hospitalization.
2. Pay all hospital bills directly.
3. Collect all original documents:
   - Discharge summary
   - All hospital bills and receipts
   - Diagnostic test reports
   - Prescription and pharmacy bills
   - Treating doctor's certificate
4. Submit claim form along with documents within 30 days
   of discharge.
5. Reimbursement processed within 15 working days of
   receiving complete documentation.

SECTION 7 — RENEWAL CONDITIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

- Policy is renewable annually without medical examination
  up to the age of 65 years.
- Renewal must be done before the expiry date to avoid
  a break in coverage.
- No Claim Bonus (NCB): 10% increase in Sum Insured for
  every claim-free year, up to a maximum of 50%.
- Grace period of 30 days is allowed for renewal after
  policy expiry; coverage lapses during grace period.
