SECTION 1 — COVERAGE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1.1 Own Damage (Accidental Damage)
Covers repair or replacement costs for damage to the
insured vehicle due to accidental collision or overturning.
Maximum limit: IDV (Insured Declared Value) of ₹8,50,000

1.2 Third-Party Liability
Covers legal liability arising from injury, death, or
property damage caused to a third party by the insured vehicle.
- Third-party bodily injury: Unlimited (as per Motor
  Vehicles Act, 1988)
- Third-party property damage: Up to ₹7,50,000

1.3 Fire and Theft
Covers loss or damage to the insured vehicle due to:
- Fire, explosion, or self-ignition
- Theft or attempted theft
- Lightning or flood damage
Maximum limit: IDV of ₹8,50,000

1.4 Personal Accident Cover
Covers the owner-driver in case of accidental death
or permanent disability while driving the insured vehicle.
Maximum limit: ₹15,00,000

1.5 Roadside Assistance (Add-On)
24x7 emergency roadside assistance including:
- Towing up to 50 km
- Emergency fuel delivery
- Flat tyre assistance
- On-site minor repairs

SECTION 4 — CLAIM PROCESS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 Accident Claims
1. Report the accident to insurer within 48 hours via
   app, website, or helpline: 1800-209-5858
2. Do NOT move the vehicle before surveyor inspection
   unless it poses a safety risk.
3. Upload clear photographs of damage via the app.
4. Surveyor will inspect within 24–48 hours of intimation.
5. Approved repairs carried out at network garage or
   reimbursement for non-network garage.

4.2 Theft Claims
1. File an FIR at the nearest police station immediately.
2. Inform insurer within 24 hours of theft.
3. Submit FIR copy, RC book, and all vehicle keys.
4. After 90-day police investigation period, insurer
   settles claim at IDV minus deductible.

Grace Period:          90 days (vehicle uninsured during this period)
Renewal Inspection:    Required if break in policy exceeds 90 days
